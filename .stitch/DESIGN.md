# Design System: PDF Free Editor
**Project ID:** 1518960248103232327

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The aesthetic is "Professional, Glassmorphic, and Highly Curated." The goal is to elevate standard PDF utilities into a premium SaaS-like workspace. It should feel deeply trustworthy, clean, and dynamic.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Primary (Purple Accent):** `#8b5cf6` (A vibrant, sleek purple defining CTAs and brand identity).
*   **Secondary (Dark Slate):** `#0f172a` (Authoritative dark grays for navigation and text).
*   **Tertiary (White Base):** `#ffffff` (Clean space for maximum readability).
*   **Ghost Accents:** Soft, subtle backgrounds (`rgba(139, 92, 246, 0.1)`) for hovering elements.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Font:** Inter (Clean, geometric, highly legible system font).
*   **Headlines:** Bold, high-contrast, utilizing subtle gradient text where applicable.
*   **Body:** Neutral gray for standard text to afford breathing room and reduce eye strain.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:** Perfectly pill-shaped (`rounded-full`), applying glowing box-shadows on hover (`0px 0px 20px rgba(139, 92, 246, 0.3)`).
*   **Cards/Containers:** Glassmorphic (`backdrop-filter`) when overlapping complex backgrounds, or solid white with soft `xl` border radius against very light gray (`#f8fafc`) app backgrounds.
*   **Inputs:** Zero hard 1px outline borders—instead use very soft border colors that transition cleanly to a bright primary ring focus.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   Generous whitespace (`padding-8` to `padding-16`) scaling up on larger screens.
*   Centralize key tools and dropzones into massive, unambiguous interaction areas.
